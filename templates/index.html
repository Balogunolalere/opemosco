Hello There

We Are Opemosco

We are Opemosco newline Nig Ltd, a contracting and consulting Company and we are engaged in diverse business activities. Our affiliation offer comprehensive services in engineering consultancy, contracting and supply of building materials.
We are a company with extensive construction and management experience, that provides its clients with experienced architects, knowledgeable engineers and professional reliable crafts-people that will help realize all of your projects' needs.

Our team will offers you the most professional construction services in Nigeria, well trained employees and construction workers that assure safe and efficient services while delivering on time and within the project's budget.

As the construction industry evolves, it's constantly evaluating and refining the methods and process used to manage construction, successfully deliver completed projects on time and within budgets. Ironically the more we look ahead to the future of the industry, the more we find ourselves looking to the past for answers. Construction has come a full circle from the master builder concept in ancient times, to a one stop shop for design back where it started with the twenty first century. We are here to serve you better ,the future is here .
